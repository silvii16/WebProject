TBU Student Begins Internship at BALFIN IT

By TBU Daily Staff April 2026

Iris Malaj, a student at Tirana Business University, has started her professional internship at BALFIN IT in the Digital Transformation Department.

Following a process of testing and selection among TBU students, Iris was chosen for the internship because of her technical skills and strong academic background.

During the internship, she will take part in real digital transformation projects and gain valuable experience in a professional environment.

This opportunity reflects the growing cooperation between Tirana Business University and BALFIN IT in supporting students’ career development in the digital sector.
