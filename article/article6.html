ANNOUNCEMENT

Sisal NEXTGen Internship Opportunity Announced for TBU Students

By TBU Daily Staff • April 2026

Tirana Business University has informed students about a new professional development opportunity through the Sisal NEXTGen internship program.

The program is designed to support and develop young talent in the fields of ICT and Business. In its first edition, Sisal NEXTGen will include 30 motivated students, who will have the opportunity to gain practical experience across more than 10 different departments.

Participants will work on real projects and receive guidance and mentoring from professionals in the field.

The internship offers students the opportunity to:

  • Develop practical skills
  • Explore their career interests
  • Build real professional experience
“Students will gain practical experience and work on real projects with professional guidance.”

Students interested in applying are encouraged to complete the application form within the next day through the link shared by the university.

EXPERIENCE

Real Projects

Participants will gain hands-on experience by working on real projects in different departments.
