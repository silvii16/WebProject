Guest Lecture on Market Research Held for TBU Master’s Students

By TBU Daily Staff • April 2026

Students of the Master’s program in Digital Marketing and Communication at Tirana Business University recently attended a special lecture in the Marketing Research course delivered by guest lecturer Silvana Xheka.

With more than 20 years of experience in market and consumer research, Silvana Xheka shared with students an in-depth perspective on the importance of this profession in strategic decision-making within organizations.

As the founder of Insight Sage Agency and a director at OSHEE Group, she presented real case studies and practical experiences from her work with international organizations, public institutions, and private companies.

During the lecture, students discussed:

  • The importance of market research in the development of products and services
  • The main types of qualitative and quantitative research studies in Albania
  • The sectors that most frequently use market research, including telecommunications, banking, FMCG, and development organizations

Particular attention was given to the profile of the Albanian consumer, their behavior and perceptions, as well as the way local trends are increasingly influenced by global developments.

“The lecture helped students connect academic theory with practical experience in today’s market environment.”

The lecture offered students a valuable opportunity to connect academic theory with practical experience and gain a deeper understanding of the current market environment.
