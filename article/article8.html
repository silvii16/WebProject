Antonio Ragusa Visits TBU and Delivers Lecture on Ferrari and Luxury Branding

By TBU Daily Staff • April 2026

Tirana Business University recently welcomed Antonio Ragusa, Dean of Rome Business School, for an official visit and open lecture with students.

The visit began with a strategic meeting between the two institutions, aimed at strengthening academic dialogue and exploring future opportunities for cooperation.

The highlight of the visit was the open lecture titled “Ferrari: Driving Luxury Beyond the Road,” during which students examined how Ferrari has gone beyond being simply a car manufacturer to become a global symbol of excellence, exclusivity, and innovation.

Through the Ferrari case study, students discussed key topics such as luxury branding, strategic positioning, and the creation of long-term value in international markets.

“Ferrari has become much more than a car manufacturer — it is a global symbol of luxury and innovation.”

The lecture offered students valuable global perspectives and an opportunity to connect business theory with real-world examples from one of the world’s most recognized luxury brands.

TBU expressed its appreciation to Antonio Ragusa for sharing his expertise and highlighted its intention to continue cooperation and future joint initiatives with Rome Business School.
