ANNOUNCEMENT

TBU Publishes the Examination Schedule for the Second Semester

By TBU Daily Staff • January 2026

Tirana Business University has announced the examination schedule for the second semester of the 2025–2026 academic year for students of the Faculty of Information Technology.

The exam session will take place from 2 February to 13 February 2026 and includes the following study programs:

  • Business Informatics
  • Computer Engineering
  • Software Engineering

Students are advised to carefully check the date, time, and classroom for each exam according to their study program and year of study.

“On the day of the exam, all students must bring their identification card.”

The full examination schedule has been shared with students via email and the respective class groups.

TBU wishes all students success in their exams.
