TBU Opens Applications for Student Grants

By TBU Daily Staff • April 2026

Tirana Business University invites graduating high school students to apply for the TBU Grant, a financial support opportunity for those who wish to continue their studies at the university.

The grant selection process is based on four main evaluation components:

  • Academic average
  • Motivation letter
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Interview

According to the university, the TBU Grant is designed to support motivated and talented students and help them pursue higher education at one of the country’s most promising universities.

“The TBU Grant is designed to support motivated and talented students and help them continue their academic journey.”

Students interested in applying can find more information through the Admissions Office and the official TBU communication channels.

SELECTION

Evaluation Criteria

Students will be evaluated based on academic performance, motivation, extracurricular activities and interview results.
